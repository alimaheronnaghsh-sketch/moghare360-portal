# MOGHARE360 Copyright and Asset Policy

## Purpose

This policy governs assets used in MOGHARE360 ERP demo, commercial showcase, and release packaging.

## Rules

1. **No third-party copyrighted assets without license** — Images, icons, fonts, and media from external sources require documented permission or license before use.
2. **No car brand logos without permission** — Benz, BMW, Toyota, Lexus, Kia, Hyundai, and other automotive brand logos are prohibited unless explicitly licensed.
3. **No copied website text** — Marketing copy, labels, and descriptions must be original or owner-provided.
4. **No unlicensed fonts** — Commercial font files are prohibited unless license is documented in the Asset Registry.
5. **Owner-provided Moghareh Motors logo** — The user-provided Moghareh Motors logo at `public_html/assets/moghare360-brand/moghareh-motors-logo.jpg` is treated as an owner-provided asset.
6. **Asset registry required before commercial use** — All visual assets must be registered in `erp-asset-registry.php` before commercial demo or external presentation.
7. **Screenshots** — Must be captured from owned/local product pages only.
8. **Demo package** — Must include only owned or product-generated assets.
9. **Font files** — Font files are prohibited in the repository unless license is documented.

## Approved Font Stack (CSS only)

```
Vazirmatn, Tahoma, Segoe UI, Arial, sans-serif
```

## Prohibited Without License

- Car manufacturer logos and trademarks
- Stock photography from third-party sites
- Icon packs without documented license
- Copied UI from other ERP or SaaS products
