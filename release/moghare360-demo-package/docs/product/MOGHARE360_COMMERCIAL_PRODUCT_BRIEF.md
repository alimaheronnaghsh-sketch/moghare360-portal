# MOGHARE360 Commercial Product Brief

## Product Definition

MOGHARE360 is a **Repair Shop Operating System** for Iranian auto repair workshops — internal ERP, business-ready reporting, and commercial demo packaging. It is **not** production SaaS in Phase 10.

## Target Customers

- Single-branch repair workshops (Starter / Standard)
- Professional multi-service workshops (Professional)
- Workshop networks evaluating enterprise rollout (Enterprise Ready — design only)

## Core Value Proposition

- Unified customer → operation → inventory → finance preview → CRM → HR flow
- RTL Persian UI, Soft Run tested foundation
- Management reporting without official accounting

## Modules (Phases 1–9)

Customer Core, Operation Engine, Rule Engine, Inventory & Purchase, Financial Preview, CRM, HR, UI Productization, Business Ready Reporting.

## Boundaries

- No production login/auth/permission changes
- No real SaaS, billing, payment gateway, or tenant activation
- No customer portal public login
- No official tax/final invoice

## Not Production SaaS

Phase 10 delivers **commercial demo readiness** only. Multi-tenant production requires explicit future approval.
